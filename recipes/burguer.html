Burguer

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun.

Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Ingredients:

  • half tablespoon olive oil.
  • 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped.
  • 500g pack British Beef Steak Mince.
  • 1 tablespoon mixed dried herbs.
  • 1 egg, beaten.
  • 4 slices mature Cheddar.
  • 4 white rolls.
  • a few rounds of lettuce leaves, torn.
  • 1 beef tomato, sliced.
  • Condiments like ketchup to serve.

Step by Step Guide:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened and starting to turn golden. Set aside.

  2. In a bowl, combine the beef mince with the herbs and the egg. Season, add the onions and mix well. Using your hands,
    shape into 4 patties.

  3. Cook the burgers on a preheated barbecue or griddle for 5-6 minutes on each side. While the second side is cooking, lay a
    slice of cheese on top to melt slightly (if using).

  4. Meanwhile, lightly toast the cut-sides of the buns on the barbecue. Fill with the lettuce, burgers and tomato slices. Serve
    with ketchup or any other condiment, if you like.
